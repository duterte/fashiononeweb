KALEE HEWLETT

Kalee is a renowned celebrity stylist who has recently established herself and a preeminent fashion expert and TV presenter. In addition to working on feature films, television programs, commercials, and promos, Kalee has also worked for events such as Elle Style Awards, Glamour Awards, London Fashion Week, Vodafone London Fashion Weekend and more. The Style Hanger, Kalee’s YouTube channel for style advice, has more than 50,000 viewers.

MATTHEW O‘BRIEN

At just 24 years old, Matthew O’Brien has already taken the fashion industry by storm. He started his own label at just 15 and became a household name after winning the inaugural season of Britain’s Top Designer; He has since returned to the show as an Ambassador and a Judge. Premiering at London Fashion Week to rave reviews, Matt’s fantastic ability to produce contemporary aesthetics while simul-taneously using historical referencing has garnered his brand a huge following.

Q & A with Matthew >

LISA CLAYTON

From Celine to McQueen, Lisa has armed herself with a wealth of experience having worked at many of the premier global fashion houses. Lisa's contemporary and unique 'Maxi-Minimal' design aesthetic has evolved organically throughout her years in the luxury ready-to-wear market. Her undeniably distinctive style has caught the attention of many high profile personalities, resulting in her working directly with the likes of pop royalty no less than the colossal Lady Gaga. Lisa's unique insight allows her to offer a fascinating perspective on the design process, injecting invaluable guidance and feedback for the contestants on Design Genius.
