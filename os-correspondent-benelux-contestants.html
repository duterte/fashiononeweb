CONTESTANTS

Elke Sockeel

The dark horse, quiet, fashionable tuned in to the fashion scene

Elke Sockeel is 25 years of age and lives in Ghent. This girl has two Masters degree (organizational psychology and business economics-management) but is currently a freelance blogger/writer in pursuit of her passion for fashion.

Sean William Bogaers

Mr. Big Personality with no fashion experience

Sean William Bogaers is a dance instructor, project manager and promoter by profession from the Netherlands. Though he doesn't have a background in fashion, his passion for art and other people made him want to pursue this art form.

Judith Binet

Judith Binet

Judith Binet, the 27 yr old actress from Mortsel is a people person who has an easygoing personality. Though she doesn't have any experience in reporting, her passion and drive urged her to join the Fashion One Correspondent Search. Aside from acting, Judith shows her creativity as a jewelry designer and will soon launch her own web shop.

Joyce Berx

The cute and perky one

Joyce Berx from Brasschaat is a 25 yr old marketing and event management graduate to loves to communicate with people. She's an outgoing and positive person who now runs a hostess and modeling agency and will soon be launching a new web shop for her own jewelry line.

Debby Gommeren

The expert, and heavily experienced

24 yr old Debby Gommeren has worked as a model for Maxim Magazine, P-Magazine, GR8 Street Magazine, Feeling Magazine, and many more. She has walked the catwalk for Marlies Dekkers, The Client, and 2 Heaven. Debby also participated in beauty pageants such as Miss Hawaiian Tropic, Miss Earth, and Miss Civilization Of The World. She has been on TV shows such as 'Beauty And The Nerd' on VTM and 'Take Me Out' on 2Be.

Verona Verbakel

Young and unique. Tuned into fashion at an early age

Verona Verbakel is a 20-year old acting-student (third year) at Conservatory Antwerp from Ghent, Belgium. She loves swimming, horseback riding, running and fitness. Aside from sports, her hobbies are writing, travelling with her theatre company, presenting, acting in movies and series, singing, dancing and you've guessed it fashion. Verona models and keeps a fashion blog "thepaleskinnedbitch.blogspot.com".

Shylia Yvonne

Competitive and bold American Girl

Originally from Miami Beach Florida, Shylia moved to Utrecht to broaden her career as a model. This easygoing 33 yr old has done shoots of interest, Pin-up, Glamour, Editorial, Body Paint, and Artist projects to name a few. Her credits include MTV Video Music Award 2007, Pepsi, Miller, GMC/Buick, Panteen, Cartoon Network, Bacardi, and Martell to name a few.

Leonie Lagaune

Model working in Antwerp fashion scene.

Beauty and brains, this is how one can describe this 24 year old Dutch-Austrian beauty from Belgium. Leonie Lagaune has a Masters Degree in Tourism from the University of Leuven where she also got her Bachelor's degree in Communications and units in Law. She's a model by profession and has worked with brands such as Axe, Bacardi, Corona, M&M's, Samsung, BMW, Coca Cola, Volkswagen, and BMW among others. Leonie is fluent in Dutch, English, German and French, and loves fashion, soccer, cooking and traveling.

Nele De Groodt

The sweet girl

Nele De Groodt is a Communications graduate from the University of Gent. She speaks Dutch, English and French. Nele works as a model for Bardolino Bags, Christoph Vrankenne, Frank De Mulder, Mike Steegmans, Gunther Broodcoorens, Gunther Frans, Bram Blondeel, Hans H, Karen Devriese, Marnix De Paepe, Mike Van Cleven, Pablo Vicente, Ron Stam, Jan Cornelis, Dominique Cerutti, and Basesix.
