Music Portal ( Click here for the Music Portal Site )

Fashion One will only accept clips with music from our music portal. Content providers need to send an email Jeson for the access to the music portal. Content providers need to notify Jeson before using any song/music from the portal.

Search

On the top-right side of the screen you should see a search box. Type any tag or description of the song/music that you are looking for. The portal should display all results for the search query.

For example you can type things like "sexy" or "up tempo" and it will show you the songs matching those tags.

Play

On the top-left hand side of the screen you should see 'Music Player'. Click on it and all songs in the current page will be displayed. You can play any of the songs on the page.

Download Link

After reserving a song through Jeson, you can start downloading the song.

To download the songs, click on the song you want to download, then click the Download button.

You have the options to download the vocal tracks, or instrumental tracks, or the full mix song.
