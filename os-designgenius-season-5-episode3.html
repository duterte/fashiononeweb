EPISODE 3

CONSTRUCTION WORKER

Episode Challenge: Steam Punk
Accessories Mandatory Materials: Safety goggles, tool belt
Wardrobe Mandatory Materials: Construction vest, hard hat

From construction hunk to a rebel of fashion, the remaining six are getting their toolbox ready for another challenging task by turning construction safety tools into a classic steam punk ensemble. This retro-futuristic mash up of a style will be going through an overhaul, as the designers are taking DIY into the next level.
