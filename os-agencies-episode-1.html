AGENCIES

D'VISION MODEL MANAGEMENT

ELITE MODEL MANAGEMENT

Touché Models

In this episode, we kick off this season with 3 of the leading agencies in the world - D'Vision Model Management Warsaw, Elite Model Management Miami, and Touché Models Amsterdam. Check out how the likes of Cindy Crawford, Anja Rubik and Alexandra Madar got started as well as how an agency that produced two of the Top Model franchises run its empire.

EPISODE 1

D'VISION MODEL MANAGEMENT - WARSAW

Owned by Lucenya Szymanska, D'VISION Model Management is one of the biggest and most successful model agencies in Poland, established in 1997. It is the mother agency of super models like Anja Rubik and Zuzanna Bijoch. They also discovered Emilia Nawarecka, Patrycja Gardygajlo, Maja Salamon, Katarzyna Smolinska and many others.

The agency also represents Sasha Knezevic and talented actress Weronika Rosati.

ELITE MODEL MANAGEMENT - MIAMI

A Division of Elite Models NYC, Elite Model Management Miami is a friendly family-oriented agency focusing on discovering, development and introducing new talents worldwide. Aside from searching for male and female models, they also have a kids division as well as TV/Film division with Allee Newhoff as the serves latter’s director.

Joining Elite’s roster of super models as the likes of Coco Rocha, Cindy Crawford, and Gisele Bundchen, are Elite Maimi’s very own Alexandra Madar, Kristina Peric, Ditta Kucsik, Kseniya Belousova, Bojana Krsmanovic, and the fresh faces of Amanda Elizabeth, Logan Avidan and Cole Monohan.

TOUCHÉ MODELS - AMSTERDAM

Touché Model Management Amsterdam is one of the leading and most successful agencies in the Netherlands. According to agency director, Freek Koster, the agency’s strong booking table gave them 110 girls and 85 boys to represent. With the diversity of talents available, Koster launched Model House TV, a platform to see how models work and live. Model House has produced notable shows including a season of America’s Next Top Model as well as Holland’s Top Model.

Representations include: Social Media influencer Robert Mitchell, magazine editorial girl Aline Trippers, graphic designer/model Lauren Messack, and soccer player turned model, Rogier De Brujin.
