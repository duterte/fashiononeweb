8 MOST BREATHTAKING COUTURE WEDDING GOWNS

Posted on: April 13, 2015, By: Stephanie Park

Spring is here and wedding bells are in the air as designers and event planners get prepared for one of the busiest bridal seasons of the year. Just in time for all of the romance and matchmaking, author Marie Bariller is releasing a book that is dedicated to the art and design of haute couture wedding gowns––aka the dresses that wedding dreams and thousands of Pinterest boards are made of.

Couture Wedding Gowns is a compilation of some of the greatest and most original haute couture wedding gowns ever designed, and is set to hit bookstores on May 26. Can’t wait to start planning––or daydreaming––your perfect wedding? See our picks for the 8 most breathtaking couture wedding gowns in the gallery above!
