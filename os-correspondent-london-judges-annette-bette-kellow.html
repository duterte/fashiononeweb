ANNETTE BETTE KELLOW

Annette Betté Kellow is a London based writer, actress and burlesque dancer, specializing in film.

She is Winner of Best U.K at London Burlesque Week and has performed on catwalks for fashion weeks in Paris, Milan and London. She was the first person to ever perform burlesque at Norway Fashion Week for designer Fam Irvoll, which made front page news. She has performed for the likes of Prince Harry, Kate Moss and Canal Plus. She has presented for Shoreditch Radio and guest presented with Jo Good on BBC Radio London.

She has recently completed a role in Michael Winterbottom's, The King of Soho, due for cinema release in 2013. Her other films have debuted at Cannes, BFI, Venice, NY and The Guardian Newspaper event. She has been the face of commercial brands such as Colgate, Nintendo, Tennents, Mentos, Visa, ITV Celebrity Juice as well as the brand face for Mary Portas' Kinky Knickers through the Channel 4 series, Queen of Shops.
